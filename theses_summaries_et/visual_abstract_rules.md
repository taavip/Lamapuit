## Ajakirja (NAR) stiilinäide / Technical Requirements:

Please provide a Graphical Abstract meeting the following technical requirements:

- **Size:** The image should have a resolution of 600 dpi.
- **Font:** Use a sans serif font, 12–16 points
- **File type:** TIFF, JPG or PNG
- **Format:** use a 4x3 aspect ratio for your image, minimum 10x7.5cm or 4x3inches
- **Orientation:** landscape
- **Caption:** 20-30 words

### The graphical abstract should:

- be simple
- be original (no trademark or copyright images)
- use colour
- use only limited text, mainly for labels
- consist of a drawing, diagram, graph, etc. illustrating a main point or methodology of the paper
- read from top down or left to right
